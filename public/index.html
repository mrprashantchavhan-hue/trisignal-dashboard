📏 Strategy Standards & Definitions

Bollinger Bands (BB): Standard deviation (Std Dev) measures volatility. A Std Dev of 2.0 covers ~95% of price action. Period (default 20) is the lookback for the moving average.
RSI Bounds: Standard 14-period RSI. Lower bound (35-40) for oversold/entry support, Upper bound (60-65) for overbought/trend strength.
ATR Multiplier: Uses Average True Range to set Stop Loss. A multiplier of 2.5 means SL is set 2.5x the average daily volatility away from entry.
Risk/Reward (RR): Ratio of potential profit to potential loss. A 2.0 RR means the Take Profit is twice as far as the Stop Loss.
